Mac and Cheese

Baked is Better

Mac and cheese is a creamy and cheesy dish that is made with macaroni pasta and cheese sauce. It is a comfort food that can be enjoyed as a main course or a side dish. Mac and cheese is easy to make and can be customized with different kinds of cheese, toppings, and add-ins

There are many ways to make mac and cheese, such as stovetop, oven-baked, or microwave. You can also use different kinds of pasta, such as shells, penne, or rotini. The cheese sauce can be made with a roux, a bechamel, or a combination of cheese and milk. You can also add some extra flavor and texture with ingredients like bacon, ham, broccoli, peas, or breadcrumbs.

Here is a list of the ingredients you will need for a simple oven-baked mac and cheese recipe:

  • 8 ounces of elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of bread crumbs

Here are the steps to make the mac and cheese:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F and lightly grease a 9x9 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the macaroni in a large pot of boiling salted water, according to the package directions. Drain and return to the pot.
  3. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and whisk in the flour. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually whisk in the milk and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until thickened. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Stir in the cheddar cheese until melted and smooth. Pour the cheese sauce over the macaroni and toss to coat well. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it evenly.
  5. Sprinkle the parmesan cheese and bread crumbs over the top of the mac and cheese.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and the bread crumbs are golden.
  7. Let the mac and cheese rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
