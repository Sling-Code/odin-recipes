Lasagna

A Classic Italian Dish

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, cheese, meat sauce, and béchamel sauce. It is baked in the oven until golden and bubbly, and then served hot with a green salad and some bread. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that can feed a large crowd or be frozen for later use.

There are many variations of lasagna, depending on the region, preference, and availability of ingredients. Some common types of lasagna are vegetarian, seafood, spinach and ricotta, mushroom, and chicken. You can also customize your lasagna by using different kinds of pasta, cheese, and sauce. The basic steps of making lasagna are the same, regardless of the type you choose.

Here is a list of the ingredients you will need for a simple meat lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 15 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 cups of milk

Here are the steps to make the lasagna:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water, according to the package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water. Lay them flat on a baking sheet and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef, breaking it up with a spatula, until browned and cooked through. Drain the excess fat and transfer the meat to a bowl.
  4. In the same skillet, cook the onion and garlic until soft, about 5 minutes. Add the marinara sauce and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs and stir in the ricotta cheese, parsley, nutmeg, and half of the parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  6. In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter and whisk in the flour. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Gradually whisk in the milk and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until thickened. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce over the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Arrange 4 noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat with another layer of sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella. Top with the remaining sauce, noodles, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for another 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and crisp.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
