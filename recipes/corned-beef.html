Delicious Corned Beef Recipe

Sure, I can do that. Corned beef is a cured and salted cut of beef that is typically cooked with spices and served with cabbage, potatoes, and carrots. It is a popular dish for St. Patrick’s Day, but it can also be enjoyed year-round.

There are different ways to make corned beef, such as boiling, baking, or slow-cooking. You can either buy a ready-made corned beef brisket from the store, or you can make your own from scratch by brining a fresh brisket for several days.

Here is a list of the ingredients you will need for a baked corned beef recipe:

  • 1 (3-pound) corned beef brisket (with spice packet)/li>
  • 1 (12-ounce) bottle of Guinness beer
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Here are the steps to make the corned beef:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F and lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish
  2. Place the corned beef in the baking dish and add the Guinness to the pan. In a small bowl, stir together the brown sugar, mustard, and the spice packet that came with the corned beef. Rub this onto the top of the corned beef.
  3. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 4 hours, removing the cover for the final 30 minutes of cooking.
  4. Transfer the corned beef to a cutting board and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing against the grain. Serve with your favorite side dishes, such as cabbage, potatoes, and carrots.
